Quesadilla Recipe

Go back to Main Page

Description:

Quesadillas are a simple and delicious Mexican dish that consists of tortillas filled with cheese and other ingredients. They are easy to make and can be customized with a variety of different fillings.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a blend)
  • 1 cup cooked protein (chicken, beef, shrimp, or beans)
  • 1/2 cup diced vegetables (bell peppers, onions, tomatoes, etc.)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup sour cream

Steps:

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Place a tortilla in the skillet and sprinkle half of it with cheese, protein, vegetables, and cilantro.
  3. Fold the tortilla in half to cover the filling, then cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is crispy.
  4. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and fillings.
  5. Slice the quesadillas into wedges and serve with salsa and sour cream on the side.
